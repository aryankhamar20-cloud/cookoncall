Frequently Asked Questions

How does pricing work? ▾
You pay a platform fee per booking. The chef's hourly rate is included in the price shown. No surprise charges after the booking.
Can I cancel a booking? ▾
Yes, free cancellation up to 2 hours before the booking time. After that, a 20% cancellation fee applies to cover the chef's preparation time.
How does the chef get paid? ▾
Chefs receive 80% of the booking amount. CookOnCall keeps a 20% platform fee. Payouts happen within 24 hours of job completion.
Is there a refund policy? ▾
If you're unhappy with the service, contact us within 24 hours. We review each case and offer full or partial refunds depending on the situation.
What payment methods are accepted? ▾
We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), debit/credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), and cash on arrival via our Razorpay-powered checkout.

CookOnCall started with one simple idea — every Indian family deserves fresh, professional home-cooked meals without the hassle. We connect you with verified local chefs who cook in your kitchen, exactly the way you like.

Our Story

Founded in Ahmedabad in 2024, CookOnCall grew out of a personal problem. Our founder Aryan noticed that busy professionals, young families, and elderly households all struggled with the same challenge — getting fresh, home-style food without spending hours in the kitchen.

We started by connecting 5 local chefs with families in Satellite, Ahmedabad. Within 3 months we had 50 chefs and 500 happy households. Today we're scaling across Gujarat with plans for Mumbai, Delhi, and Bengaluru.

Our mission is simple: make professional home cooking accessible to every Indian household.

What We Stand For

Trust & Safety

Every chef is background-verified with Aadhar ID check before going live on our platform.

Quality First

Chefs are rated after every booking. Low-rated chefs are reviewed and removed if needed.

On Time, Every Time

Chef Welfare

Terms of Service

Last updated: March 2025

1. Acceptance of Terms

By using CookOnCall you agree to these terms. If you do not agree, please do not use our platform. These terms apply to all users including customers, chefs, and visitors.

2. Services

CookOnCall is a marketplace connecting customers with independent professional chefs. We do not employ chefs directly. Chefs are independent contractors who set their own availability and specialties.

3. User Accounts

You must provide accurate information when creating an account. Each person may only have one account. You are responsible for keeping your login credentials secure. We may suspend accounts that violate these terms.

4. Bookings and Payments

All bookings are subject to chef availability and confirmation. Payments are processed securely via Razorpay. CookOnCall charges a platform fee of 20% on each booking. The remaining 80% goes to the chef.

5. Cancellations and Refunds

  • Cancellations made 2+ hours before booking: Full refund
  • Cancellations within 2 hours: 80% refund
  • No-show by customer: No refund
  • No-show by chef: Full refund + ₹100 credit

6. Conduct

Users must treat chefs with respect. Any abusive, threatening, or inappropriate behaviour toward chefs will result in immediate account suspension. Chefs must maintain professional standards and punctuality.

7. Limitation of Liability

CookOnCall is not liable for any direct or indirect damages arising from use of our platform. We are a marketplace and are not responsible for the quality of food or service provided by independent chefs beyond facilitating the connection.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

1. Information We Collect

  • Name, phone number, email address
  • Location data (with your permission)
  • Booking history and preferences
  • Device information and IP address
  • Payment information (processed securely by Razorpay — we never store card numbers)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To match you with available chefs
  • To process bookings and payments
  • To send booking confirmations and updates
  • To improve our service and user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share limited information with chefs (name, address, booking details) only as necessary to complete your booking. We use trusted third-party services (Firebase, Razorpay, Google Maps) who have their own privacy policies.

4. Location Data

We request location access to show nearby chefs and for live tracking. Location data is used only during active sessions and is not stored permanently without your consent.

5. Data Security

Your data is stored on Google Firebase servers with industry-standard encryption. We use HTTPS for all data transmission. Access to user data is restricted to authorised team members only.

6. Your Rights

  • Request a copy of your data
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Update your information at any time
